SkateKostin International is a comprehensive training program designed by a team of experienced skating professionals dedicated to working with aspiring skaters of all levels to help them achieve their goals. Our team consists of experts in performance and presentation, jump and spin technique, and strength and agility training.

Our goal is to work with our skaters to help them reach their goals whether that is landing their first axel or competing internationally.
