Our Mission and Vision

Our vision is to provide a well-structured and rigorous training program that enables the development of a fierce competitive mindset in our athletes leading to achievement of goals for skaters of all levels from beginners to champions at the elite level. Our team consists of professionals with specialties in all areas of figure skating who have a passion for the sport that they share with their athletes.

Why Choose SkateKostin?

We know that creating successful athletes requires a coordinated team effort. And that's why we have put together the best team of figure skating professionals to work with our skaters and their families. Our program offerings are designed to provide the most effective coaching model for achieving success on the ice. Each skater receives individualized instruction as well as group instruction to maximize their on and off-ice development while working within their budget and time constraints. Families can be confident that as part of the SkateKostin team their skater will be encouraged and motivated each day to reach their personal goals.
