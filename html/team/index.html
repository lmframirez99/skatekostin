Our On Ice Coaching Team

Konstantin Kostin

Trained in Moscow, Russia, Konstantin is a two-time Latvian National Champion, five-time World Figure Skating Championships competitor and 1992 Olympian with over 20 years of coaching experience.

Konstantin is an ISU Technical Specialist recently serving on the technical panels at the 2016 Figure Skating World Championships in Boston, the 2015 and 2017 European Championships, the 2017 Japan Open Figure Skating Championships, and a number of Grand Prix and Junior Grand Prix events. Konstantin is an expert in jump technique and overall technical skating skills for athletes of all ages and abilities.

His skaters include medalists at the Regional, Sectional, National and International levels. Konstantin's focus with his skaters is to provide a comprehensive training program that covers all aspects of elite athlete development.

Contact Konstantin

Geneviève Coulombe

A former International/World Junior Pairs Competitor for Canada, Geneviève has been coaching skaters of all levels for over 18 years. An expert in all areas of freestyle skating, she specializes in spin technique and skater program presentation and packaging.

In addition to coaching on the SkateKostin team, Geneviève is responsible for organizing the individual on- and off-ice training programs for each skater, as well as providing consultation for costume design, music selection, and media communications.

Geneviève has coached numerous elite skaters in the US and Canada including the 2015 US National Intermediate Ladies Champion as well as medalists at several Regional, Sectional, National and International events.

Contact Geneviève

Sergey Meller

An experienced coach in all aspects of figure skating, Sergey is an expert in on- and off-ice harness work as well as strength and conditioning training for figure skaters. Sergey works with athletes at all levels, focusing particularly on improving air position and jump technique.

Contact Sergey

Carrie Wall

Carrie is a national, international and Olympic coach with over ten years experience. She coaches both freestyle and pairs skaters with a strong emphasis on athlete development of both body and mind to build self-confidence and teach life lessons while engaging in sport.

Contact Carrie

Dmitri Kazarlyga

Dmitri is a former international ice dancer from Kazakhstan. He competed at six World Figure Skating Championships as well as the 1994 and 1998 Olympics. He joins the SkateKostin team as an expert in moves in the field, skating skills and ice dance.

Colonial Figure Skating Club

Based at Nashoba Valley Olympia in Boxborough, MA, Colonial Figure Skating Club offers a wide range of figure skating training programs including learn to skate, moves in the field, freestyle, dance, pairs and synchronized skating. CFSC counts among its skaters the 2014 Juvenile Girls National Champion and the 2015 Intermediate Ladies National Champion.

Visit Colonial Figure Skating Club

Nashoba Valley Olympia

Located on the Acton/Boxborough line with three sheets of ice, Nashoba Valley Olympia is a centrally-located training facility with ample ice time for training as well as multiple off ice rooms for dance, physical conditioning and off ice harness work.
